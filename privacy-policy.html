LocaSync Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

At LocaSync, we value your privacy. We collect data you provide—like addresses, photos (up to two per entry), folder names, and notes—to help you save and organize spots.

We use Google Maps and Places for location services, which may collect search queries and device location (optional). Firebase may track usage for analytics, and Google Sign-In uses account info for login.

We don’t share your data except with these services as needed. Edit or delete your data anytime in the app.

Contact: support@locasyncapp.com
